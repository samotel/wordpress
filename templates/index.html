ABOUT US
SAMOTEL TOURS

Samotel Tours and Travels was founded by Chef Samson Clement "Okongo". We offer tour guide services for Safaris, cultural tours, hiking, and fishing. Find us so that we can take you through beautiful sceneries in South Sudan and the Larger East Africa (Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania and Congo)

Photography

Photo travel is based on Cultural diversity, our heritage, the big five in our wildlife, Bird viewing, naming, and definition of characteristics. Gorilla trackings and other apes' families.

Hiking

Hiking and trekking on Mt Kinyeti, Imatong, Buyaa/Larim hills, Mt Kilimanjaro - Tanzania and The Seven Hills of Kampala City.

Tours

Cultural tours and safaris - Boma, Badinglo, Nimule National Parks, Masai mara national Park, The Serengeti, and Queen Elizabeth National Park - Uganda.

Fishing

Fishing at Sudd swamp, along the White Nile and Blue Nile, Jinja the Nile source in Uganda.
